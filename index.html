Welcome to Geita Girls Secondary School

Geita Girls Secondary School is a premier government institution dedicated to providing high-quality secondary education for girls. We offer an environment structured to foster discipline, strategic leadership skills, and elite academic mastery.

About Our Institution

Founded on principles of empowerment and academic distinction, Geita Girls Secondary School accommodates students across both Ordinary Level (Form 1 to 4) and Advanced Level ranges. Our comprehensive educational environment aims to maximize potential in the fields of Science, Mathematics, and Language streams.

Our Vision

To be an elite academic powerhouse that cultivates outstanding integrity, self-reliance, and innovation among young global citizens.
